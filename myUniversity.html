About BMCC

Welcome to Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC)

Located in the heart of New York City, BMCC embodies the bustling energy of Tribeca's cultural hub, the dynamic spirit of Wall Street, and the enduring promise of the Statue of Liberty. Here, every student is not just a learner but a vital part of our diverse community.

At BMCC, we live by the motto "Start here go Anywhere" to champion accessibility and excellence, providing affordable, high-quality education that prepares students for real-world success. Our campus thrives on innovation and supports every student's journey of self-improvement, offering ample opportunities for those eager to excel.

As a Minority and Hispanic-Serving Institution, BMCC proudly welcomes students from the New York City area and abroad, creating a vibrant tapestry of global perspectives. We're committed to breaking barriers to education, ensuring that every student has the tools and support they need to achieve their dreams.
